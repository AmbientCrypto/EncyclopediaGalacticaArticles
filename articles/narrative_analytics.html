Building upon the theoretical bedrock established in the preceding section, which illuminated the confluence of narrative theory, computational linguistics, data science principles, and psychological perspectives, we now turn to the practical implementation of these insights through diverse methodological approaches. The choice of method in narrative analytics is seldom a simple matter of preference; it is intrinsically linked to the specific research questions posed, the nature of the narrative data itself (be it a single complex novel, thousands of social media posts, or transcribed interviews), and the desired depth and breadth of understanding (whether seeking statistical trends across populations or nuanced interpretation of individual voice and experience).
