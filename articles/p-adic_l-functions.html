6.1 Overconvergent Modular Forms

Katz’s approach originated from a deceptively simple shift in perspective: rather than focusing solely on the Fourier coefficients or the Galois representations attached to modular forms, he returned to their geometric roots ‚Äì modular forms as functions on elliptic curves with level structure.
